# AI Voice Synthesis — Research Notes

## Key Players
| Company | Model | Speed | Quality | Use Case |
|---------|-------|-------|---------|----------|
| ElevenLabs | Turbo v2.5 | Real-time | Studio | Voice cloning |
| OpenAI | TTS-1 | <100ms | Podcast | API integration |
| Cartesia | Sonic | Very fast | Natural | Streaming |
| Coqui | XTTS | Medium | Open source | Self-hosted |

## Key Insight
Voice is becoming a commodity. The differentiator is:
1. Latency (<200ms for real-time)
2. Emotional range (not just words)
3. Voice cloning quality (few samples)

## Opportunities
- Voice memos → structured notes
- Voice UX for note-taking
- Multi-language with same voice
